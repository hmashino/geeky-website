Character

Mario

He is the representative character of Nintendo and the protagonist of the "Super Mario" series. Known for his red cap and overalls, he embarks on adventures using his jumping skills and power-up items.

Luigi

Mario’s twin brother. He is characterized by his green cap and overalls, is slightly taller than Mario, and possesses excellent jumping abilities. Despite his reserved nature, he shows great courage.

Princess Peach

The princess of the Mushroom Kingdom. Although she is frequently kidnapped by Bowser, some works portray her as taking part in adventures herself. Her elegance and kind heart are among her most appealing traits.

Bowser

Mario’s archenemy, known for his turtle-like appearance and immense strength. He schemes to conquer the Mushroom Kingdom and, at times, introduces twists to the story.

Yoshi

A companion inspired by a dinosaur. With his long tongue, he can grab enemies and even throw eggs, supporting Mario throughout his adventures.

Toad

A resident of the Mushroom Kingdom who serves Princess Peach loyally. Although small in stature, he is agile and endearing.
